Root 16

For my advanced higher project I created a digital version of the well known game, "Connect Four". It was created with Visual Basic and allowed two people to play against each other. Once the game had finished, it would ask the winner for their name and store their details in a leaderboard.

The leaderboard would display the winner's name, colour, the amount of discs they had placed and the date on which they won. It was sorted by number of discs ascending and would only show the top 10 winners. It also displayed who had won most recently.
